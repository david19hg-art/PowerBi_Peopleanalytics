# PowerBi_Peopleanalytics
Interactive Power BI dashboard for People Analytics, providing insights into employee turnover, demographics, and organizational performance

- Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze key workforce metrics to better understand the organization’s performance. This includes evaluating employee headcount, turnover rate, deficient contracts, dismissals, and overall workforce composition.

-Tools Used
- Power Query (data cleaning and transformation)  
- Power BI (data modeling and visualization)  

- Process
1. Cleaned and prepared the dataset using Power Query  
2. Combined and structured data tables for analysis
3. Developed DAX measures to calculate employee age, identify deficient contracts, and generate key HR metrics for analysis   
4. Built interactive visualizations in Power BI  
5. Analyzed results to extract key insights  

Leveraged AI tools to accelerate development, validate DAX calculations, and improve overall analytical accuracy


Key Results

- The company has a total of 93 employees**, consisting of 39 women and 54 men, providing a clear view of workforce distribution

- The employee turnover rate is 18.28%, indicating a moderate level of workforce rotation that may require further analysis  
- The Cashier area shows the highest employee turnover, indicating a potential need to review retention strategies in this department
  
- A total of 6 employees (5.45%) were classified as deficient contracts, meaning they left the company within a short period under justified dismissal conditions. This may highlight potential inefficiencies in the hiring or onboarding process.  

- The age distribution shows:
  - Minimum age: 22
  - Average age: 36
  - Maximum age: 61 

- The total monthly payroll amounts to $109,375, providing insight into the company’s labor cost structure.  


## 📌 Conclusion
This analysis provides a comprehensive overview of workforce dynamics, helping identify key areas such as employee turnover, hiring effectiveness, and workforce distribution. These insights can support data-driven decisions to improve organizational performance.
